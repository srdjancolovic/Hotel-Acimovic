About

Hotel Acimovic is located in a quiet town area Rastoci. It's 800 m away from the city center. Also, it's location is excellent due to proximity of coastal cities. Dubrovnik is 30 km away and Herceg Novi is 40 km away. In addition to the accommodation service, the hotel has a cafe with a capacity of 40 seats, as well as a terrace with a capacity of 50 seats. For hotel guests there is parking provided in front of the hotel, as well as a garage with 10 parking spaces. Wi-Fi signal is available in entire hotel, as well as elevator.

    • Rooms

    Info

    The hotel has 26 rooms, maximum capacity of 80 beds. Rooms can be single, double, triple and quadruple. Rooms have a separate bathroom. There is a minibar available in every room. Each room has a Wi-Fi signal. Some rooms have a balcony with a beautiful view of the longest sunset in the city and a city river.
